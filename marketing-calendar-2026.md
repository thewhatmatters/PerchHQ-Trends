## Usage Notes

**For PerchHQ Sparks:**
- Trigger spark generation 3-7 days before each event
- Use `last30days` skill to research trending content angles
- Cache research in `sparks.research_summary` field
- Generate 3-5 content ideas per event with copy-paste hooks

**Content Format Priority:**
1. Short-form video (Reels/TikTok/Shorts)
2. Carousels (educational)
3. Stories (behind-the-scenes)
4. Static posts (quotes, announcements)

**Research Keywords by Category:**
- **Food:** recipes, viral food, comfort eating
- **Wellness:** mental health, self-care, fitness
- **Cause:** awareness campaigns, fundraising, education
- **Holiday:** traditions, celebration ideas, gift guides
- **Fun:** humor, trends, relatable content
