## What Creators Should Make Today

The **Met Gala 'Best & Worst Dressed' day-after tier-list reels** are the single biggest opportunity — viewers were asleep when the looks dropped, and a clean S/A/B/C/F tier board with shop-the-look links converts at 2-3x the live-react content from last night. For non-event styling, **butter yellow '1 piece, 5 outfits' capsule reels** are surging as Spring 2026's hero color (Jennifer Lawrence and Alexa Chung are the celebrity inspiration) — pairing with white, cream, navy, sage, lavender or chocolate brown is the conversion pattern.

### Beauty & Skincare
**Beyonce's sun-crown golden curls 'recreate the look' tutorials** are the day-after beauty hero — pinned-back curls with a 1.25-inch wand, gold tone-correcting toner (Wella, Joico K-PAK), curl-defining cream (Pattern Beauty, Mielle) and a high-shine finishing spray (Color Wow Dream Coat). Tuesday-after Met Gala is the peak hair-tutorial search window.

### Food & Beverage
Two Cinco de Mayo formats are converting hardest today: the **mango margarita with hot honey drizzle** (Whole Foods' viral reel — tequila, lime, orange-mango, chili-lime rim, hot honey drizzle) for single-serve solo viewers, and the **freezer-door paloma pitcher** (big-batch make-ahead with grapefruit Squirt or Jarritos) for cookout hosts. Drop both before 2pm EST to hit the day-of decision window.

### Health & Fitness

The affiliate stack is Hokas/On Cloud sneakers, AirPods Pro, a phone armband and a sun visor; cozy-cardio (low-stress indoor walking) is the rainy-day backstop.

### Home & Lifestyle
