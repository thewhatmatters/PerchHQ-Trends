Friday before Mother's Day means two parallel content waves are live: the **last-call gifting affiliate push** (Mejuri stacking, Sunday-delivery cutoffs) and the **Memorial Day on-ramp** (swimwear, pre-summer fitness, festival prep). ## Format watchlist

Two formats showed up across multiple categories and are worth flagging as cross-vertical patterns creators can apply:

- **The 'cheap dupe of the expensive thing' split-screen** — works for Bondi Sands ($19 vs $38), e.l.f. cloud skin (drugstore vs prestige), Honey Bear ($7 SLP fix), freezer fine dining ($22 vs restaurant), IKEA + Bemz slipcover ($80 vs $4K).
- **The before/after intentional-living reel** — works for friction-maxxing (phone → record player), sniffari (zoomies → calm), 25-day Pilates (Day 1 → Day 25), Honey Bear (sippy-cup → straw).

If a creator is producing for multiple verticals, building a single template around either format and reskinning per niche should compound this week.
