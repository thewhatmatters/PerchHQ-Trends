## Why this Saturday matters

Today is Mother's Day Eve (T-1 from May 10). The window for Sunday-delivery has closed for most creators' audiences, so the dominant creator format pivots from "order this with two-day shipping" to "make this tonight," "pick it up at the mall today," or "do this together with mom tomorrow morning." Memorial Day weekend (May 23–25) is also T-2.5 weeks out — pre-summer body, swimwear, and grilling-prep formats are warming up but haven't peaked yet.
