# PerchHQ Trends Report — 2026-05-17

## Summary

13 creator-relevant trends identified across all 10 categories. Today's standout signal is TikTok GO — the platform's new in-app travel booking feature that launched May 12 and gives travel creators a direct commission path. Across other categories, Japanese walking continues its 2,986% search spike as the year's defining fitness format, #PerfumeTok is the top TikTok Shop category, and summer beverage content (dirty sodas, coconut iced coffee, proffee) is ramping hard.

## Category Breakdown

### Travel & Hospitality
- **TikTok GO in-app booking** (viral_moment, confidence 0.95): The biggest platform development this week. Travel creators can now tag destinations and earn commissions on bookings through Booking.com, Expedia, and Viator directly inside TikTok. This is a new monetization layer — expect a rush of destination-reveal and hidden-gem content optimized for bookable tags.

### Health & Fitness
- **Japanese walking method** (content_format, confidence 0.92): Alternating 3 min brisk / 3 min gentle walking for 30 minutes. Eugene Teo's explainer crossed 10M TikTok views. Walk-along, before-and-after, and "I tried it for 30 days" formats are all performing. Low production barrier makes this accessible for any fitness creator.

### Beauty & Skincare
- **Medicube Collagen Night Wrapping Mask** (product, confidence 0.88): Overnight mask with strong wake-up reveal potential. Creators are filming before-bed application + morning transformation content.
- **LED face masks** (product, confidence 0.86): Beauty-tech crossover continuing to drive iron-man-selfie and skin transformation series content. Premium price point ($100–$400+) but strong affiliate potential.

### Beauty & Skincare / Fashion & Apparel
- **#PerfumeTok** (hashtag, confidence 0.90): 600K+ posts and counting. Fragrance is the #1 TikTok Shop product category. Blind-reaction, dupe comparison, and layering tutorial formats are all working. Cross-tags into fashion for scent-to-outfit pairing content.

### Food & Beverage
- **Dirty soda + proffee** (content_format, confidence 0.87): Colorful layered pours with high visual payoff. Dirty sodas (soda + cream + syrups) riding MomTok momentum; proffee (protein + coffee) tapping the fitness-food crossover.
- **Coconut iced coffee** (content_format, confidence 0.85): 2026's summer signature drink. Tropical-looking layered pours using coconut milk/cream — easy daily content for food creators.

### Jewelry & Accessories
- **Bracelet stacking + charm jewelry** (product, confidence 0.85): Bracelet stacking has overtaken ring stacking. Wrist-of-the-day reveals, charm customization hauls, and bold colorful pieces are outperforming minimalist content.

### Home & Lifestyle
- **Wax melting lamps** (product, confidence 0.84): Replacing traditional candles as the ambient home product. Room-tour reveals and ASMR wax-melting close-ups are the primary content formats.

### Tech & Gadgets / Home & Lifestyle
- **Mini projectors** (product, confidence 0.83): Under-$100 projectors driving bedroom-to-theater transformation and outdoor movie night content. Strong before-and-after dark-room reveal format.

### Baby & Kids
- **Nee Doh sensory toys** (product, confidence 0.83): Continuing to sell out and restock on TikTok Shop. Satisfying squeeze videos, unboxing, and gift-guide formats for parent creators.

### Pets
- **Flying bird toys** (product, confidence 0.82): Realistic flapping bird toys driving hilarious pet reaction and chase videos. High shareability from the visual comedy of pets leaping after the birds.

### Fashion & Apparel
- **Soft luxury + coquette aesthetics** (content_format, confidence 0.88): Neutral co-ords, textured knitwear, bows, and lace are the dominant spring micro-aesthetics. OOTD and capsule wardrobe content built around these frameworks.
