## Category Breakdown

### Food & Beverage
Two trends today. **Behave candy** is the standout story — a near-bankrupt low-sugar candy brand that went viral on TikTok Shop, sold out Super Sour Skulls in three days, and just landed in ~2,000 Target stores. This is a powerful case study for creator-commerce content. **Coconut iced coffee** is ramping as the signature summer drink format, with photogenic layered pours driving engagement.

### Beauty & Skincare
**Skincare empties season** is in full swing with Prequelskin, cocokind, and Dieux Skin getting repeat mentions in mid-year holy-grail roundups. **LED face masks** continue their crossover into beauty-tech, with the futuristic visual hook making them prime unboxing and glow-up timeline content. The **#WhatsGoingOn** sound trend also opens a door for beauty transformation reveals.

### Health & Fitness
**Owala FreeSip** water bottles have transcended hydration to become collectible fashion accessories, with limited Color Drop releases selling out in minutes and Rose Quartz unboxing videos hitting 1.4M likes. Creators are building entire collection and ranking content around colorways.

### Fashion & Apparel
**Boho and 70s-inspired aesthetics** are aligning across runways and TikTok — linen sets, embellished jeans, giant sunglasses, and ball heels. Strong content angles include runway-to-street translations and thrift-flip boho looks. The **#WhatsGoingOn** duo trend is also being used for outfit reveal transitions.

### Jewelry & Accessories
**Bracelet stacking and mixed-metal layering** is the dominant spring jewelry trend, with creators filming stacking tutorials and affordable-vs-luxury comparison content. Bold statement pieces are replacing the minimalist aesthetic.

### Tech & Gadgets
Two product trends: **handheld gimbal stabilizers** for mobile filmmaking (strong before/after demo content), and **smart pet feeders/GPS collars** crossing into pet-tech review territory. LED masks also tag into this category.

### Baby & Kids
**Sensory toys** — Ooey Gooey, Nee Doh Cubes, and 3D-printed fidget dragons — are dominating kids content. ASMR squish compilations and parent honest-review formats are the top content plays.

### Pets
**Smart pet gadgets** (automatic feeders, GPS collars, health wearables) are growing as a content niche, with setup tutorials and vacation-mode feeding routines performing well.

### Home & Lifestyle
**Wax melting lamps** are replacing traditional candles as the cozy home aesthetic upgrade, with their warm glow making them perfect for room tour and aromatherapy content. Owala's Color Drops also cross-tag here as lifestyle accessories.

### Travel & Hospitality
**Organized travel duffels** with shoe compartments and compression cubes are driving satisfying packing content ahead of summer travel season. Pack-with-me and carry-on-only challenge formats are performing well.
