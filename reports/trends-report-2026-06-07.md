## Highlights

The throughline today is texture and engineered engagement. On BeautyTok, "cloud skin" is overtaking glass skin as the soft-focus, filter-free finish, while spicule "micro-needle" serums drive first-reaction texture content. Food has swung firmly anti-sweet: "savory-maxxing" (pickles, salty plates) is beating dessert, even as a high-protein yogurt tiramisu holds on as the one sweet exception. Several trends are formats deliberately built to farm comments and duets — the "Greece or Turkey?" travel debate, the playlist-driven "treadmill strut," and "CatPOV" first-person pet cameras. Summer styling stays maximalist and mixed: coastal cowgirl and #blokette on FashionTok, dopamine decor on HomeTok, and bracelet stacking "arm candy" on JewelryTok.
