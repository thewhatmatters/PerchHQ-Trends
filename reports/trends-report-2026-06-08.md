## Highlights

Today's throughline is "soft pastels and storytelling maximalism." Color is doing the heavy lifting in beauty and fashion: butter yellow has become NailTok's new neutral (search demand up ~1,043%), paired with the cool-girl cat-eye manicure, while affordable PDRN ("salmon DNA") serums keep the skincare glow story going. The dominant styling mood is more-is-more — '90s multi-layered tops and an indie-grunge boho revival on FashionTok, maximalist charm necklaces replacing minimal pendants on JewelryTok, and "grandma-core" collected interiors beating beige on HomeTok. Food leans easy-and-healthy with crispy chicken shawarma (ASMR-friendly) and no-bake frozen yogurt fruit clusters. Format trends reward specificity and participation: the 120-second plank as a duet-bait challenge, and hyper-local "one stall the locals line up for" travel content outperforming broad city guides. Two reliable product reviews round it out — the Theragun Mini 2 for wellness creators and the Yoto Player for screen-free parenting.
