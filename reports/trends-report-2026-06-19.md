## Highlights

Today's throughline is "cheap, comfortable, and easy to film." Beauty cools off literally and figuratively — "cloud skin" replaces high-glow dewy looks with a soft-blur finish that survives summer heat, while tingly spicule serums sell texture results without harsh acids. Fashion leans playful and sporty: the Blokette aesthetic mixes jersey tops with flippy skirts, and statement colorful flip-flops swap minimalist neutrals for brights. Food goes light and tropical — Biscoff yogurt tiramisu is the high-protein no-bake dessert of the season, and coconut iced coffee is the cafe-copycat pour replacing plain cold brew. FitTok consolidates around "hybrid training" for people who won't pick a lane, HomeTok upgrades filming backgrounds with $20 sunset lamps, and Tech crowns the wireless mini projector as the impulse gadget. ParentTok goes screen-free with the Toniebox, PetTok beats the heat with cooling beds and ice mats, Travel sells "destination dupes" for budget trips, and Jewelry goes bold and personal with chunky beaded pieces and the storytelling "First Date Necklace."
