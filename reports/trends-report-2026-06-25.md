# PerchHQ Daily Trends Report — 2026-06-25

**Batch:** `trends-2026-06-25` · **Trends:** 16 · **Categories covered:** 10/10 · **Expires:** 2026-06-28

Creator-relevant signal was strong across every niche today. Summer seasonality dominates: cooling gadgets, SPF, iced drinks, and pet heat-relief products all spiked. Below is the per-category rundown with the actionable creator angle for each.

## Beauty & Skincare

Beauty of Joseon Rice Sunscreen continues its run as SkinTok's no-white-cast summer SPF — best as a "perfect under-makeup SPF" or "no white cast on deeper skin tones" demo. Cushiony tinted lip oils are the other strong product story, carried by close-up glossy-application clips ("lip glow-up" reveals, shade hauls).

## Fashion & Apparel

Two content-format trends: layered-tops maximalism (sheer-over-denim, texture clash — "style one tank 5 ways") and the evolving coastal-cowgirl 2.0 aesthetic (western shirts over swimsuits, turquoise/shell accents — thrift hauls and beach-to-rodeo transitions).

## Food & Beverage

Three viral recipes/drinks: yogurt tiramisu (anti-sweet, high-protein dessert taste test), coconut iced coffee (the tropical successor to cold brew — barista-vs-dupe), and the Sleepy Girl Mocktail (cross-tagged with Health & Fitness as a nightly wind-down ritual tracker).

## Health & Fitness

Smart rings vs smartwatches is the wearable head-to-head creators are filming (30-day recovery experiments). The Sleepy Girl Mocktail also lands here as a wellness ritual.

## Home & Lifestyle

Sunset lamps (cheap room/background transformation) and portable mini ACs (summer cooling "does it work?" tests) are the two product stories — both lean on budget appeal and satisfying setup clips.

## Tech & Gadgets

Divoom Ditoo pixel speaker (custom pixel-art + cozy desk-setup content) leads; smart rings and mini ACs cross-tag in from Health and Home. All framed around consumer-gadget reviews, not industry news.

## Baby & Kids

Screen-free audio players (Yoto Player, Toniebox) are the recurring parent-creator endorsement — "screen-free summer" reviews and Yoto-vs-Toniebox comparisons.

## Pets

Summer-driven: cooling pet beds (heat-relief reaction videos) plus the cat-POV camera content format (first-person "cat adventure" edits pulling tens of millions of views).

## Travel & Hospitality

Underrated Southeast Asia (Laos, lesser-known Philippines spots) is the most-shared escape — "skip Bali, go here" itineraries and budget day-in-the-life content.

## Jewelry & Accessories

Maximalist charm necklaces and colorful stacked/beaded pieces are taking over as "quiet luxury" fades — storytelling reveals ("each charm means something") and layering tutorials.

## Notes & Methodology
