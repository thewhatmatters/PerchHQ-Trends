# PerchHQ Daily Trends Report — 2026-06-26

**Batch:** `trends-2026-06-26` · **Trends:** 15 · **Categories covered:** 10/10 · **Expires:** 2026-06-29

Peak-summer seasonality continues to drive creator-relevant signal across every niche today: heat-relief products (misting fans, cooling pet mats), anti-sweet food, recovery and "snack-sized" wellness, and layered/maximalist summer fashion and jewelry. Below is the per-category rundown with the actionable creator angle for each.

## Beauty & Skincare

Medicube's Collagen Night Wrapping Mask is the strongest product story — an overnight "glass skin" payoff that films well as a before-bed-to-morning reveal or a week-long glow-up diary. K-beauty barrier-repair formulas continue to dominate SkinTok discovery.

## Fashion & Apparel

Two content-format trends carry the category: multi-layered tops maximalism (the "style one tank 5 ways" / more-is-more revival) and coastal cowgirl 2.0 (western shirts over swimsuits, turquoise and shell accents — beach-to-rodeo transitions and thrift hauls).

## Food & Beverage

Yogurt tiramisu (yogurt + Lotus Biscoff + coffee) leads the anti-sweet, high-protein dessert wave — no-bake layering plus first-bite reactions. Best as a "does it taste real?" taste test or a 5-minute summer recipe reel.

## Health & Fitness

The Theragun Mini 2 is the recovery gadget creators say actually delivers — post-workout and desk-tension demos, "recovery tools ranked" reviews, and 30-day experiments.

## Home & Lifestyle

Handheld misting fans are the go-to summer heat-hack product ("surviving the heatwave" POVs, travel essentials). Mini projectors cross-tag with Tech for cozy "galaxy ceiling" room-setup and home-cinema content.

## Tech & Gadgets

Mini projectors (shared with Home) and the Mi Smart Band 9 lead — the latter going viral on its 21-day battery life, fueling "cheap band vs. expensive smartwatch" head-to-heads. All framed as consumer-gadget reviews, not industry news.

## Baby & Kids

The Legendairy Milk Honey Bear straw cup is the recurring parent-creator feeding win ("worth the hype vs. skip" reviews, straw-training demos). DIY craft and slime kits anchor the "screen-free summer" angle with satisfying make-along content.

## Pets

Summer-driven: cooling pet mats are meme-worthy again via "dog collapse" reaction clips, and the AquaPurr fountain remains the picky-cat hydration fix with hundreds of millions of views ("will my cat finally drink?" before/afters).

## Travel & Hospitality

"How much a week in [destination] actually costs" receipt-breakdown videos are the save-magnet format — specificity (real hotel, meal, transport prices) is the whole appeal.

## Jewelry & Accessories

Maximalism over minimalism: charm necklaces and stacked beaded bracelets lead ("build a charm necklace with me," stack hauls), with permanent welded anklets a strong summer coastal/mermaid-core moment (the welding process itself makes satisfying short-form).
