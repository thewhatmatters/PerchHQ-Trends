## Beauty & Skincare

Two product stories. ## Health & Fitness

Bed-cooling systems (Chilipad-style water-cooled pads) are the recovery-sleep upgrade as heatwaves wreck sleep — "7 nights with vs. without" sleep-score experiments and honest "recovery tools worth it" reviews. Cross-tagged to Home & Lifestyle.

## Home & Lifestyle

Dopamine-decor gallery and plate walls anchor the category — warm, storied, maximalist 2026 interiors made shareable as a "renter-friendly gallery wall under $50" time-lapse. Bed-cooling (shared with Fitness) also lands here as a summer bedroom upgrade.

## Tech & Gadgets

3-in-1 magnetic charging stands are the consumer-gadget pick: the satisfying magnetic "snap," the cable-clutter-to-clean-desk payoff, and a sub-$50 price. Framed as ASMR snap close-ups and desk-setup reviews, not industry news.

## Baby & Kids

## Pets

3-in-1 dog water bottles (water + treat compartment + poop-bag/scraper) are the heatwave walk gadget — "testing it on a 90-degree day" demos and summer dog-walk-essentials POVs.

## Travel & Hospitality

"Destination dupe" videos are the most-saved planning format: pair an overcrowded hotspot with a cheaper lookalike (Siargao over Palawan, Jeju over Bali) in a fast split-screen with real cost callouts.

## Jewelry & Accessories

Beaded bracelet stacking has overtaken ring stacks — chunky beads, shells and color-mixing feeding the coastal/mermaid-core aesthetic. Best as a "build a summer bead stack with me" make-along or a "one stack, beach-to-dinner" restyle.
